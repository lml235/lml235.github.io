Made with 11/0 Toho Glass Seed Beads and 4mm glass flat-rounded beads

Collection 1 - Obsidion, Pearl, Bicone

Collection 1 is comprised of three bracelets. Obsidion is a black and gold bracelet made with 11/0 Toho gold seed beads and black stone with accented gold coloring. Pearl is a bracelet made with imitation pearls. Despite that fact it still maintains a substantial weight and feel. It is an original design that can be easily made. The tutorial an be found @xiao.lin.li on instagram. Finally, Bicone is a staggered bicone design that is easy and elegant made with glass bicone beads in either 6/0 or 8/0 and 11/0 Toho seed beads. Design is made by Tezukuri Notes!on Youtube.
